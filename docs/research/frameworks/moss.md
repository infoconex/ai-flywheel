## Working Hypothesis

MOSS is expected to provide strong prior art for using execution evidence to drive persistent source-level improvements.

The main distinction to investigate is whether adaptation has one dominant destination, source modification, or whether MOSS also models multiple execution layers and routes different weaknesses to different forms of persistent change.

## Evidence to Collect

- The adaptation pipeline.
- The role of production failures or execution traces.
- Candidate generation and validation mechanisms.
- Rollback and safety mechanisms.
- Whether procedures and runtime judgment are independent adaptation targets.

## Provisional Position

Do not claim evidence-driven self-modification as unique to the AI Flywheel. Focus comparison on multi-layer evolution routing and responsibility allocation.
