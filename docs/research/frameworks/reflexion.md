# Reflexion Research Dossier

> **Research status:** Working dossier. Findings should be validated against primary sources before being treated as final comparative conclusions.

Evaluate Reflexion as prior art for agents that use feedback from previous attempts to improve subsequent behavior through persistent reflection.

## Working Hypothesis

Reflexion is expected to provide strong prior art for using outcome feedback and persistent linguistic memory to improve future attempts.

The principal comparison is whether learning remains primarily in reflective memory or can be deliberately routed into executable code, procedural control, validation, or retained runtime reasoning.

## Evidence to Collect

- Types of feedback signals used.
- Structure and persistence of reflection memory.
- How reflections influence future action.
- Whether lessons become executable or procedural assets.
- Whether the framework explicitly chooses among multiple adaptation destinations.

## Provisional Position

Do not claim learning from failure or persistent lessons as unique to the AI Flywheel. Focus differentiation on multi-layer persistence and evolution routing.
