# Reflexion Research Dossier

> **Research status:** Working dossier. Findings should be validated against primary sources before being treated as final comparative conclusions.

## Research Objective

Evaluate Reflexion as prior art for agents that use feedback from previous attempts to improve subsequent behavior through persistent reflection, and examine how human feedback or authority participates in that process.

## Working Hypothesis

Reflexion is expected to provide strong prior art for using outcome feedback and persistent linguistic memory to improve future attempts.

The principal comparisons are whether learning remains primarily in reflective memory or can be deliberately routed into deterministic capability, procedural control, validation, or governance, and whether autonomous behavior is constrained by explicit human authority boundaries.

## Evidence to Collect

- Types of feedback signals used.
- Structure and persistence of reflection memory.
- How reflections influence future action.
- Whether lessons become executable or procedural assets.
- Whether the framework explicitly chooses among multiple adaptation destinations.
- The role of human feedback, approval, or authority.

## Provisional Position

Do not claim learning from failure or persistent lessons as unique to the AI Flywheel. Focus differentiation on multi-destination persistence, evolution routing, and the governance model.
