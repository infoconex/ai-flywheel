# Principle 2 Research: AI Is the Operator

## Principle Under Review

The AI executes the operational process and can create, invoke, interpret, and improve the capabilities it uses to perform the work.

See the canonical [principles specification](../../specification/principles.md#principle-2-ai-is-the-operator-not-merely-the-assistant).

## Prior-Art Question

How established is the pattern in which an AI agent is the direct consumer and operator of tools, scripts, code, and procedures that it or another AI generated?

## Working Assessment

This principle is expected to have substantial prior art. Autonomous agents, coding agents, tool-using agents, and iterative agent loops already place AI in an operational role.

The AI Flywheel should therefore avoid presenting AI operation itself as the primary novelty claim.

## Evidence Needed

- Examples of AI creating a capability and later invoking it.
- Examples of persistent agent-owned tools or skills.
- Whether execution is autonomous, supervised, or human-triggered.
- Whether the AI merely uses tools or also owns improvement of the operating process.

## Differentiation Question

The more defensible question is not whether AI can operate tools, but whether AI operation is integrated with bounded human authority, the full evidence-driven improvement cycle, and the Moving Determinism Boundary.
